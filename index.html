Welcome to Animal Farm: An Interactive Guide

Explore Orwell's Classic Allegory

Published in 1945, George Orwell's Animal Farm is a powerful allegory that reflects events leading up to the Russian Revolution and the Stalinist era of the Soviet Union.

Through the story of farm animals who rebel against their human farmer, Orwell explores themes of totalitarianism, corruption, and the manipulation of language for political control.

"All animals are equal, but some animals are more equal than others."

This interactive guide helps you explore the characters, themes, and historical parallels that make Animal Farm one of the most important political satires ever written.
